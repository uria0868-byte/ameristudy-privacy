📋 1. Overview

AmeriStudy AI ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application that helps people prepare for the U.S. naturalization civics test. We take your privacy seriously and collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service.

This Privacy Policy applies to the AmeriStudy AI mobile app available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

✅ Short version We do not sell your data. We do not store your voice recordings. Your study progress is saved locally on your device. No account or login is required to use the app.

🎙️ 3. Microphone & Voice Data

AmeriStudy AI requests access to your device's microphone only when you use the voice answer feature in Car Mode. Here is exactly what happens when you record a spoken answer:

  1. Your spoken answer is recorded locally on your device as a short audio clip (typically 5–15 seconds).
  2. The audio clip is encrypted and transmitted to our secure server for transcription using an AI speech-to-text service (Whisper).
  3. The transcribed text is evaluated by an AI language model to determine whether your answer is correct, partial, or incorrect.
  4. The result (verdict + feedback) is returned to your device and displayed on screen.
  5. The audio clip is deleted from our server immediately after transcription. It is never stored, logged, or used for any other purpose.
🔒 Your voice is never stored or shared. Audio data is processed transiently in memory and discarded. We do not build voice profiles, train models on your voice, or share audio with any third party beyond the transcription service.

You can use all other features of the app (Test Mode, Learning Mode, AI Mock Interview, Rewards) without ever granting microphone access. The microphone is strictly optional and only used in Car Mode's voice answer feature.

⚙️ 5. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing the service: Displaying your progress, streaks, rewards, and personalized study recommendations.
  • Improving your learning: Identifying which questions you answer incorrectly so the app can prioritize them in future sessions.
  • Voice answer evaluation: Transcribing and grading spoken answers in Car Mode to provide hands-free feedback.
  • AI Mock Interview: Simulating a USCIS interview conversation and providing personalized coaching feedback.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, behavioral tracking, or any purpose unrelated to your citizenship test preparation.

🚫 6. Data Sharing & Selling

✅ We do not sell your data. Ever. Your study progress, voice recordings, and usage data are never sold, rented, or traded to any third party for commercial purposes.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • AI processing partners: Audio and text are shared transiently with AI service providers (Whisper, LLM) solely to process your request. These providers do not retain your data.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority, we may disclose information. We will notify you to the extent permitted by law.
  • Business transfer: If the app is acquired or merged, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users in advance.

🗓️ 7. Data Retention

Since all study progress data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of how long it is kept:

  • Study progress, scores, streaks, rewards: Stored on your device indefinitely until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
  • Voice audio: Deleted from our servers immediately after transcription (typically within seconds). Never retained.
  • AI interview session data: Discarded from server memory when the interview session ends. Not stored in any database.

To delete all your data, simply uninstall the app from your device. No account deletion request is needed because we do not maintain user accounts.

AmeriStudy AI is designed for adults preparing for the U.S. naturalization civics test. The app is intended for users aged 18 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will take steps to delete that information.

⚖️ 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to access: Request a summary of what data we hold about you.
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of any data we hold. For local device data, uninstalling the app achieves this immediately.
  • Right to opt out of voice features: You may deny microphone permission at any time in your device settings. All other app features remain fully functional.
  • California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.
  • EU/EEA residents (GDPR): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in the Contact section below.
